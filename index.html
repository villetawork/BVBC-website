Welcome To

BVBC

Baptist Voice Bible College, is a private school that offers foundational and higher education. It has been operated and managed by Grace Baptist Church since 2000. The Basic Education Department utilizes the Accelerated Christian Education system that was started in the early 1970s by dedicated parents who desired their children reared under the teaching of God’s word – the Bible. A.C.E. penetrated over 130 countries, where parents desire their children’s lives patterned after the timeless principles of God’s Word.
